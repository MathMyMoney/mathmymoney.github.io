About Zach, Founder & Content Creator

Zachariah Goldenberg has been a math nerd since the early 90s, he loves to solve problems and help people learn stuff.

Zach attended Plymouth State University where he double majored in Mathematics and Political Science. After graduating he has worked as a licensed securities trader at Fidelity Investments, an actuary at Liberty Mutual, and as a solar sales consultant at Tesla. He's currently teaching high school math and picked up his masters in data analytics to boot!

Actions speak louder than words. Zach started tutoring in middle school and volunteering in high school. Thousands of hours of volunteer work both domestically and internationally along with taking a major pay cut to begin teaching show what he is all about. Math My Money is one way he's trying to make the world a better place through education. However cliché it may sound, empowering people financially allows them to pursue their dreams and it becomes a snowball effect.

All that aside, Zach's known to make a mean pizza along with the occasional giant snowman. He lives in New Hampshire with his wife and son.
